History

Keyboard were known as typewriters and was used for data entry for computing. The keyboard we all use today is evolved from QWERTY keyboards- the first successful typewriter in 1870s. Besides paper and pen, keyboard is probably the most popular universal input tool. However its output is affecting our life.

Phenomenon-Information Overload

Every single APP pushes dozens of latest news, deals and messages each minute. Letters and words are all typed out by keyboard, this leads to anxiety and difficulty in making decisions when one’s daily life is bombarded with messages, emails, pop-up news, daily headlines, etc. The flood of information piling up on a day to day basis produces more pain than gain.

Collage

To portray a feeling of exceedingly fragmentation of information overload. The absolute chaos weakens people's desire to read the information, even if they want to, there is no way of processing and finding out the ones they are interested in.
